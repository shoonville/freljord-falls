❄ AURORA ICE FLOE · LAST CUB AFLOAT WINS ❄

Ride your polar bear cub across the frozen floe. The rim is brittle — hit an edge once and it cracks, twice and it crashes into the sea, shrinking the floe.

Grab power-ups dropped from the sky, press SPACE to dash and slam rivals off the edge, and press SHIFT to use a held item. Last cub afloat — or the fullest bar at 0:00 — wins!
